Thai Green Curry

Here's how to make some good food (even if you're English!)

So, here's what you'll need :

  • a lime
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • thai green curry paste
  • ground corriander
  • 400g of coconut milk
  • chicken breast (diced)

Alas! The cooking! Follow the instructions below nerd :

  1. chop the garlic and roast them in a pan until golden
  2. add the thai green curry paste, stir
  3. pour in the coconut milk
  4. add the ground corriander and chopped chicken breast, then cover with anothe pan
  5. serve with boiled rice
